What are SFR’s Services?



The know-how of the SFR company



The SFR company has several skills in the field of telecommunications and digital services. Here are some of its main know-how:

  • Telecommunications network: SFR has an extensive and efficient telecommunications network, including fiber optic, cable and mobile telephone infrastructures. The company is capable of providing communications services broadband and connectivity to all its customers.


  • Technological innovation: SFR invests in research and development to remain at the forefront of technological innovation in the field of telecommunications and digital services. The company develops new technologies and services to meet the changing needs of its customers.

the services provided by SFR

Security Services


SFR offers security solutions to protect devices connected to the internet against viruses, malware and attacks.



Domestic services


SFR offers solutions to control and automate home equipment (lighting, heating, alarm, etc.) remotely.



Cloud services


SFR offers online storage services (cloud) to save and share files.



Fixed Internet


SFR offers fixed internet access offers via optical fiber, ADSL or VDSL. These offers generally include unlimited landline telephony.

External services that SFR can call upon

SFR can call on subcontractors such as: the company Arvato Services with whom SFR has decided to transfer one of its call centers (Poitiers site, 571 people) and also SR Teleperformance with whom it has transferred two of its call centers (Lyon sites, 582 people, and Toulouse, 724 people). This decision corresponds to what in economics is called outsourcing: an activity previously carried out within the company is entrusted to an external company. Apart from its subcontractors, SFR relies on its collaboration with Cisco Webex.
